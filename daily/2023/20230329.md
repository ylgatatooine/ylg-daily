[Pause Giant AI Experiments for 6+ months: An Open Letter](https://futureoflife.org/open-letter/pause-giant-ai-experiments/)

Call on all AI labs to immeditely pause for 6+ months the training of AI systems more powerful than GPT-4. Powerful AI systems should be developed only once we are confident that their effects will be positive and their risks will be manageable. 
It's been signed most notable AI experts in labs, professors, entrepreneurs like Elon Musk. The signing was put on hold due to the high demand after gathering 1187 signatures.
